# Data-Analysis-Dashboard
**Annual Store Sales Analysis Dashboard
Project Overview**
This project features a comprehensive Annual Sales Performance Dashboard developed in Excel to analyze e-commerce retail data. The primary objective was to transform raw sales data into actionable business intelligence, focusing on customer demographics, regional performance, and order fulfillment logistics.

**Key Features & Visualizations**
**Order vs. Sales **Trends: A dual-axis combination chart tracking monthly revenue (Sum of Amount) against order volume (Count of Order ID) to identify peak performance months.

**Customer Segmentation:** Pie and Bar charts analyzing sales by gender (Men vs. Women) and age groups (Adult, Senior, Teenager) to pinpoint the core customer base.

**Regional Performance:** A horizontal bar chart identifying the Top 10 States by sales, with Telangana and Maharashtra emerging as leading regions.

**Operational Metrics:** Breakdown of order status (Delivered, Refunded, Cancelled) showing a 98% delivery success rate.

**Platform & Category Analysis:** Comparison of sales across various e-commerce sites (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.) and specific product categories.

**Technical Workflow
Data Preprocessing:** Cleaned raw datasets to ensure consistency in date formats, currency, and category naming conventions.

**Feature Engineering:** Grouped age data into segments (Adult/Senior/Teenager) and extracted monthly periods for time-series analysis.

**Data Modeling:** Utilized Pivot Tables to aggregate complex data points across multiple dimensions.

**UI/UX Design**: Integrated Interactive Slicers (Month, Channel, and Category) allowing users to filter the entire dashboard dynamically.

**Tools Used**
Microsoft Excel: Advanced Pivot Tables, Power Query, and Data Visualization.

Data Cleaning: Handling null values and standardizing regional names for accurate reporting.
